Philippines Tourism FAQ

What are the visa requirements for visiting the Philippines?
Visa requirements vary depending on your nationality. Please check the official website of the Philippine Embassy or Consulate in your country for the latest information.
What is the best time to visit the Philippines?
The best time to visit the Philippines depends on your interests. The dry season (November to April) is generally the most popular time to visit, while the rainy season (May to October) offers lower prices and fewer crowds.
What are some popular tourist destinations in the Philippines?
Some popular tourist destinations in the Philippines include:
  • Boracay
  • El Nido
  • Banaue Rice Terraces
  • Intramuros (Manila)
  • Cebu City
  • Siargao
What is the local currency in the Philippines?
The local currency in the Philippines is the Philippine Peso (PHP).
What is the official language in the Philippines?
The official languages in the Philippines are Filipino and English.
